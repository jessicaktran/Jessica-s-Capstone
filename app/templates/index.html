OUR VISION

Everyone deserves to receive the same trust and quality of care regardless of who they are.


We are determined to help members of the Oakland Community, especially underserved neighborhoods, low-income, non-native English speakers. It will allow them to learn about the quality of care that is provided at different hospitals, learn about common symptoms and health conditions they have, but are unaware of, and communicate their needs more effectively and directly with healthcare professionals and administration. This app addresses the lack of culturally competent care, community involvement in the transformation of healthcare, and helps to reduce the fear, anxiety, and distrust in healthcare.
